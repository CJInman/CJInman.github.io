About Me

I am an astrophysics researcher specializing in radiative transfer, galaxy modeling, and the evolution of stellar systems. My work involves combining computational simulations with observations to uncover the physical mechanisms shaping galaxies across cosmic time.

Research Interests

  • Radiative transfer in galaxies
  • Stellar population modeling
  • Multiwavelength galaxy analysis
  • Cosmology and galaxy evolution
  • Computational astrophysics
